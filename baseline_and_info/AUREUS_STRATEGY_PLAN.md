# 🏦 Aureus Systematic 3.0: Sniper-Trend Framework

The 3.0 framework is a **Trend-Following Mean-Reversion Model**. It solves the "Buy-the-Dip" trap by ensuring we only enter trades in the direction of the dominant H1 trend.

---

## 💎 1. The "Aureus 3.0" Indicators
- **EMA 200 (Trend):** Price above EMA 200 = Bullish (Buys Only). Price below = Bearish (Sells Only).
- **RSI 14 (Momentum):** Used to find "Over-extended pullbacks" within the main trend.
- **ATR 14 (Volatility):** Dynamic filter to avoid entering during extreme news spikes or zero-liquidity sessions.

## 🛠 3. Execution Rules
- **Entry:** Price must be at a 5-day structural extreme (High/Low) + RSI Overstretch + **Trend Alignment**.
- **Risk:** Fixed 1.0% per trade ($10 on a $1,000 account).
- **Exit:** Fixed TP (90 pips) or RSI Early Exit **only if in profit**.
